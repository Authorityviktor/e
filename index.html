Welcome to IUOKADA.Kofa
The Student Information and Registration Portal of the Igbinedion University, Okada, Edo State, Nigeria

Kofa is part of the West African eUniversity Project (WAeUP). It is a powerful and comprehensive Student Management System which will become the single access point to educational resources and information to all Igbinedion University Students.

Kofa controls application and student registration processes and provides comprehensive and transparent information about study progress.


Students are encouraged to make their payment of fees and other sundry charges via their Student Record
1. Select Payment from your Menu
2. Create the Payment type you desire to pay
3. Select Payment Gateway - Etranzact or Interswitch (Pay via ATM Card)
4. Follow the process and enter relevant ATM Card details or else Enter Entanzact PIN upon successful payment in bank
5. Payment for Application Fee can also be made by Bank Draft Payable to
Igbinedion University
Okada, Edo State
